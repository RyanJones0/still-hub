Construction Specification

Body

Tubular knit — no side seam. Eliminates seam twist and chafe. Premium standard (Sunspel, Lady White Co.).

Hem

Single needle chain stitch, ⅝" from edge. Creates signature rope-roll — impossible on industrial coverstitch.

Neckband

⅞" self-fabric ribbed neckband. Lower and finer than athletic 1" standard. Sits 2 finger-widths from collarbone.

Stitch density

Minimum 12 SPI on all seams. Reinforced at shoulder seams. Garment-washed pre-delivery.

Fabric: 230gsm Pima cotton, tubular knit. Sourced from Piura Valley, Peru. Handpicked long-staple fibre — softer and stronger than commodity cotton. Pre-shrunk (max 3% post first wash). Reactive dyeing, ISO 105 wash-fastness 4–5 minimum.

Fit Intent
Contemporary relaxed. 4–5" chest ease. Extended body (29" at M — 1.5" longer than standard) prevents ride-up. Slight natural shoulder drop (0.75" each side) allows fabric to hang cleanly without armhole tension. Mid-bicep sleeve — the premium menswear benchmark. Not slim, not boxy. Reference: COS organic cotton tee, Buck Mason standard tee.

Construction Specification

Shoulder

Slight drop shoulder (¾" each side). 380gsm terry requires this to hang cleanly — natural shoulder causes armhole pull at this weight.

Hood

14" height — functional, not decorative. Under 12" falls back when raised. Flat-braid cotton drawcord. Gunmetal metal aglets — no plastic tips.

Cuffs & Hem

2.5cm ribbed hem band and sleeve cuffs in matching French terry rib. Cuff snug enough to hold drawcord position at wrist.

Hardware

YKK Excella metal aglets. Flat-braided drawcord, not round tubular. Gunmetal only — no chrome or silver hardware.

Fabric: 380gsm French terry, loopback construction. Sourced from Lyon, France. Heavier than the 280–320gsm used by most competitors — the extra weight is the tactile differentiator. Garment-washed pre-delivery. Reactive dyeing, ISO 105 wash-fastness 4–5 minimum.

Fit Intent
Relaxed — 7–8" chest ease. The 380gsm weight provides structural drape at this ease — it won't look sloppy because the fabric holds its shape. Body length 27.5" sits at mid-hip, covering the trouser waistband by ~1". The hoodie is designed to be worn as a set with the Foundation Track Pant — same French terry, matching ribbing, matching hardware. Reference: Studio Nicholson, Sunspel.

Construction Specification

Waistband

3.5cm wide elastic waistband — wider than commodity standard (2cm). Holds position without constant drawcord use. Flat-braid cotton drawcord. Gunmetal metal aglets — no plastic.

Ribbed Cuff

9cm (3.5") ribbed ankle cuff in matching French terry rib. Must terminate AT ankle bone — not above. Specify inseam measured to bottom of cuff, not cut edge.

Seams

Double-stitched crotch seam (2 pass minimum). Bar tacked at all pocket openings and stress points. Minimum 12 SPI throughout.

Back Pocket

YKK #5 moulded coil zip. Pocket sits inside back right leg panel. Zip pull: gunmetal metal, matching hardware. No exposed zip tape at opening.

Fabric: 380gsm French terry, loopback construction. Same bolt as Foundation Hoodie — specify matching fabric for set cohesion. Garment-washed pre-delivery. Reactive dyeing, ISO 105 wash-fastness 4–5 minimum. Waistband elastic: 3.5cm wide flat elastic, minimum 80% recovery after 50 wash cycles.

Fit Intent
Relaxed through seat and thigh. The 380gsm French terry gives structure — this is not a shapeless sweat pant. The silhouette tapers to a ribbed ankle cuff, which is the defining detail: the cuff must sit cleanly at the ankle bone. Paired with the Foundation Hoodie, this is the STILL winter set — sold as separates but designed and photographed together. The drawcord hardware, ribbing weight, and colourway must match exactly between hoodie and track pant.
